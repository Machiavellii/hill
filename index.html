Igalo Hill Apartments

“In love with Montenegro”
______________

Our cosy apartments are designed with plenty of space for pleasant holiday with spectacular sea view on Boka Bay and surrounding mountains, enough close to the beaches, villages and walking area.
Igalo Hill apartments are located in quite environment, far enough away from the city noise but close enough to the beaches. We offer 3 accommodation units with a total of 21 beds. All units are equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, and Wi-Fi internet and fully equipped for a pleasant stay.
The nearby surroundings feature supermarket and local shop. It takes 26 km to Dubrovnik airport and 27 km to Tivat airport.
We can arrange a tour to the main attractive destinations, boat rentals, airport pick ups, daily trips and another services. Friendly hosts willing to help you with reliable advice regarding quality holiday.
